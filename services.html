Services

Black Hat Defense LLC provides authorized offensive security services designed to help organizations understand, validate, and reduce real-world security risk. All engagements are conducted under explicit authorization, with clearly defined scope and objectives.

Our work emphasizes realistic attack scenarios, measurable impact, and practical remediation guidance that supports informed decision-making.

Penetration Testing

Penetration testing simulates real-world attacks against applications, networks, and systems to identify exploitable weaknesses.

This service helps organizations:

  • Validate the effectiveness of existing security controls
  • Identify vulnerabilities with real impact
  • Prioritize remediation based on actual risk

Deliverables include a detailed report outlining findings, impact, and recommended remediation steps.

Adversarial Security Testing

Adversarial security testing evaluates how an organization would be affected by a motivated attacker operating under realistic constraints.

This approach is used to assess:

  • Viable attack paths and chaining opportunities
  • Gaps in detection and response capabilities
  • Potential operational or business impact

Testing is performed in a controlled, authorized manner and aligned to client objectives.

Network & Infrastructure Security Assessments

Network and infrastructure security assessments evaluate exposure across internal and external environments, including access controls, segmentation, and configuration weaknesses.

These assessments are commonly used to:

  • Understand external attack surface exposure
  • Review internal trust boundaries
  • Identify misconfigurations and high-risk access paths

ICS / SCADA Security Assessments

ICS and SCADA security assessments focus on identifying security weaknesses in operational technology environments where availability and safety are critical.

Engagements are designed to:

  • Minimize operational risk
  • Respect uptime and safety constraints
  • Identify exposure at the network, protocol, and configuration level

Testing is carefully planned and coordinated with relevant stakeholders.

OSINT & Attack Surface Mapping

OSINT-driven attack surface mapping identifies publicly accessible information and assets that could be leveraged by an attacker.

This includes:

  • Internet-facing systems and services
  • Exposed credentials or sensitive metadata
  • Organizational exposure across third-party platforms

The goal is to reduce unintended visibility and external risk.

Engagement Standards

All services are provided under the following principles:

  • Explicit written authorization is required
  • Scope, timing, and objectives are defined in advance
  • Testing is conducted ethically and legally
  • Findings are documented clearly and responsibly

Black Hat Defense LLC does not conduct unauthorized testing or malicious activity.
